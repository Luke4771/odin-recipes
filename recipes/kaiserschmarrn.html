Kaiserschmarrn

Ingredients

  • Flour: All-purpose flour for the batter.
  • Eggs: Separated, with yolks and whites beaten separately.
  • Milk: Whole milk to create a smooth batter.
  • Sugar: Granulated sugar for sweetness.
  • Vanilla: Vanilla extract or vanilla sugar for flavor.
  • Raisins: Optional, for texture and sweetness.
  • Butter: For cooking and giving a golden crust.
  • Powdered Sugar: For dusting the finished Kaiserschmarrn.

Instructions

  1. Separate and beat eggs: Separate the egg yolks and whites. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  2. Mix batter ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together flour, milk, sugar, egg yolks, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Add egg whites and raisins: Gently fold the beaten egg whites and optional raisins into the batter.
  4. Cook batter: In a large skillet, melt butter and pour in the batter. Cook until the bottom is golden brown.
  5. Flip and tear: Use spatulas to tear the pancake into smaller pieces. Flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.
  6. Serve: Transfer the torn pancake to a plate, dust with powdered sugar, and serve with fruit compote or applesauce.
