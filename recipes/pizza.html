Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough: Store-bought or homemade
  • Pizza sauce: Tomato sauce or marinara
  • Cheese: Mozzarella, cheddar, or your favorite type
  • Toppings: Pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, olives, etc.
  • Olive oil: For brushing
  • Herbs and spices: Oregano, basil, red pepper flakes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Roll out the dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Prepare the crust: Place the dough on a pizza pan or stone. Brush the crust with olive oil.
  4. Add the sauce: Spread a layer of pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Add the cheese: Sprinkle a generous amount of cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add the toppings: Arrange your favorite toppings over the cheese.
  7. Season: Sprinkle herbs and spices like oregano, basil, and red pepper flakes over the toppings.
  8. Bake: Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Slice and enjoy: Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
